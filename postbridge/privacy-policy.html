1. Introduction

PostBridge ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the PostBridge mobile application (the "App").

Developer: PostBridge Inc.
Contact: privacy@postbridge.app
Website: https://postbridge.app

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and password (or Sign in with Apple credentials)
  • Social Account Connections: OAuth access tokens for Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest — stored encrypted; we never store your passwords
  • User Content: Post drafts, captions, scheduled post data, and uploaded media (images/videos)
  • Communications: Support messages and contact information when you reach out to us

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers, mobile network info
  • Usage Data: Features used, screens visited, scheduling activity, interaction patterns
  • Log Data: App crashes, system activity, IP address, error logs

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media Platforms: Profile data (username, follower count) and post analytics via official APIs
  • Apple Sign-In: Apple-provided user identifier and optional email

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features
  • Create and manage your account
  • Schedule and publish posts to your connected social accounts on your behalf
  • Generate AI-powered caption suggestions and best-time-to-post recommendations
  • Display unified analytics from your connected social accounts
  • Send notifications when posts are published or fail
  • Monitor and analyze usage to improve the App
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activities
  • Process subscription payments via Apple In-App Purchases

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

4.1 Social Media Platforms (At Your Direction)

When you schedule a post, we publish content to your connected social platforms via their official APIs. This is at your explicit direction.

4.2 Service Providers

  • Cloud Infrastructure (server hosting and media storage)
  • Firebase Crashlytics or Sentry (crash reporting)
  • Firebase Analytics (aggregate, anonymized usage data)
  • Apple Push Notification Service — APNS (push notifications)
  • Apple In-App Purchase / StoreKit (subscription processing)

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or valid legal process.

4.4 Business Transfers

In a merger or acquisition, personal information may be transferred with advance notice.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Until account deletion (+ 30-day recovery window)
  • Published post history: 12 months
  • Uploaded media files: Until deleted by you, or 90 days after publishing
  • OAuth tokens: Until you disconnect the account or token expires
  • Crash/log data: 90 days

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Update: Edit account info in App settings
  • Disconnect Social Accounts: Profile → Connected Accounts
  • Delete Account: Profile → Delete Account (data removed within 30 days)
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe link in emails
  • Data Portability: Email privacy@postbridge.app

PostBridge is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided information, contact us at privacy@postbridge.app.
